Before Robotics 88, I spent four years as a lead instructor for [General Assembly](https://generalassemb.ly/) where I taught data science and full-stack web development. I gained a knack for explaining complex things clearly to people with minimal technical backgrounds. 

When I'm not on the computer I'm fronting my band [Astro Alloy](https://www.instagram.com/astro.alloy). I've also gotten into building custom guitars and analog audio electronics.
